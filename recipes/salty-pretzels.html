Salty Soft Pretzels

Description

Soft pretzels are a delicious and classic snack that originated in Germany. These soft, chewy, and slightly salty pretzels are perfect for snacking or for serving at parties. With just a few simple ingredients, you can make these pretzels in the comfort of your own home. Serve them warm with mustard or cheese sauce for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 4 1/2 to 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup baking soda
  • Coarse salt, for sprinkling

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes until the mixture becomes foamy.
  2. Add 4 1/2 cups of flour and salt to the yeast mixture and stir until a dough forms. If the dough is too sticky, add more flour, 1 tablespoon at a time, until it forms a smooth ball.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes on a floured surface.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a damp cloth. Let the dough rise in a warm place for about 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. In a large pot, bring the 4 cups of water and baking soda to a boil.
  7. Roll the dough into long ropes, about 1 inch in diameter. Shape into pretzels.
  8. Boil each pretzel in the baking soda mixture for 30 seconds on each side.
  9. Place the pretzels on the prepared baking sheet. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with coarse salt.
  10. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
  11. Serve warm with mustard or cheese sauce for dipping. Enjoy!
