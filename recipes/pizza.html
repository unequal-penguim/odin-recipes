Pizza

Description

Pizza Margherita is a classic Neapolitan pizza made with simple, fresh ingredients and is said to be the pizza that represents the colors of the Italian flag. The combination of juicy tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and olive oil creates a burst of flavor in every bite. This pizza is an all-time favorite and is perfect for those who love traditional and authentic Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz. fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 8-10 basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or a baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Slice the mozzarella cheese and place on top of the sauce.
  6. Rip the basil leaves into smaller pieces and sprinkle on top of the cheese.
  7. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza.
  8. Sprinkle salt on top.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  10. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes.
  11. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy!
